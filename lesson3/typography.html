Heading Font: Roboto Slab

This font creates a uniform view on the page and it is simple but at the same time modern.

Paragraph Font: Montserrat

This font match to the other is not confusing, it's easy to recognize that both are different and that helps to create harmony on the view. Adding to this the font creates a feeling of trust.
